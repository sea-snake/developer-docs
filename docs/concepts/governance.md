<!-- Content Brief -->
Explain the governance model of ICP. Cover the Network Nervous System (NNS) for protocol governance, SNS (Service Nervous System) for dapp governance, neuron staking and voting, proposals and their lifecycle, and tokenomics basics (ICP token, voting rewards). Focus on what developers need to know: how to decentralize their dapp with an SNS, and how NNS proposals affect their canisters.
